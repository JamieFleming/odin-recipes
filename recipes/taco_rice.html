High Protein Cheesy Chicken Taco Rice

Description

High protien cheesy chicken taco rice with 55g protein per portion with only 530kcal - bursting with flavour and perfect for meal prep.

Store in the fridge for up to 5 days - reheat in the microwave covered by a damp paper towel for 3-4 minutes.

Quantity Ingredients

1 Diced Red Pepper
1 Medium Diced Onion
4 Cloves Garlic
30g Light Butter
1 Packet Taco Seasoning
220g Salsa
200g Washed White Rice
400ml Chicken Bone Broth
120g 50% Less Fat Cheddar
Garnish Tomato
Garnish Green Onions
Garnish Parsley
