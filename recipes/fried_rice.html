High Protein Beef & Broccoli Fried Rice

Description

High protien beef & broccoli fried rice with 30g protein per portion with only 200kcal - recipe makes 4 portions. Great for building muscle.

Store in the fridge for up to 5 days - reheat in the microwave covered by a damp paper towel for 3-4 minutes.

Ingredients

Beef

Quantity Ingredients
800g Lean Steak Strips
30ml (2tbsp) Soy Sauce
15ml (1tbsp) Rice Wine
1tsp Salt
1tsp Black Pepper
15g Cornstarch
1tsp Baking Soda

Rice

Quantity Ingredients
2 Shallots
6 Cloves Garlic
300g Broccoli
650g Day Old Cooked Jasmine Rice
30ml Soy Sauce
2tbsp Oyster Sauce
2tsp Sesame Oil
15g Sweetner
15g Chilli Crisp
1tsp White Pepper
Garnish Sesame Seeds
Garnish Crispy Onions
Garnish Chilli Flakes
